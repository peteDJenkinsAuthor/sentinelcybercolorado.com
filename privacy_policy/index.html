Privacy Policy

At Sentinel Cyber Security Consulting, we treat your data with the same technical rigor we apply to our security assessments. This policy outlines how we handle information collected through our website and professional communications.

>​1. Information We Collect

>​We only collect data that is voluntarily provided by you or necessary for the security of our infrastructure:

Contact Information: Name, email address, and company details provided via our inquiry forms.

​

Technical Data: To defend our site against SQL injection and XSS attacks, we may log IP addresses and browser fingerprints to identify and block malicious activity.

​2. Data Minimization & Retention

​Purpose-Driven: We only use your data to respond to your inquiries or fulfill contractual obligations.

​Short-Term Storage: For general inquiries, we retain your data only as long as necessary to facilitate communication. Once a request is resolved or an engagement ends, data is purged according to our secure disposal protocols.

​3. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use tracking cookies for marketing or advertising. Your data is only shared if required by law or to protect the safety and integrity of our systems during a verified security incident.

​4. Security Measures

​As a cybersecurity firm, we implement industry-standard protections, including:

    ​
  • Encryption: All data sent via our contact forms is encrypted in transit (TLS).
  • ​Access Control: Only authorized personnel (SecOps) have access to client inquiry data.
  • ​Integrity: We monitor our site 24/7 for unauthorized access attempts or data tampering.

5. Your Rights

​You have the right to request a copy of the data we hold about you or to request its immediate deletion. To do so, please contact ginger.office@sentinelcybercolorado.com
